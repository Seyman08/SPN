About Us

About Us

Sexual Purity Network is a non-profit movement dedicated to promoting sexual purity, godly values, and youth empowerment. We help young people build confidence, character, and purpose through mentorship, counseling, and community programs.

Mission

To equip youth to embrace sexual purity and live out their values with courage.

Vision

A generation transformed by integrity, purpose, and faith.

Let us go forward in this battle fortified by conviction that those who labour in the service of a great and good cause will never fail.

WHAT IS
SEXUAL
PURITY
NETWORK?

Sexual Purity Network website is designed to inform, support, and empower youth and individuals committed to sexual purity. It will serve as a hub for mentorship, counseling, training, and volunteer opportunities.
Founded by Toyin Afolabi, SPN has reached hundreds of individuals across Nigeria and beyond, creating safe spaces for honest conversations and growth.

MILESTONES & ACHIEVEMENTS

Since our launch in 2022, Sexual Purity Network has remained committed to shaping a generation that values purity, purpose, and personal growth. Here’s a look at how far we’ve come:

  • Reached thousands of young people through community programs and online campaigns globally.
  • Launched and sustained our Mentorship Program, guiding teens and young adults with personalized support and accountability.
  • Hosted impactful events and webinars including Purity Hangouts and our annual Feed the Street outreach.
  • Provided financial and material support to students in need — enabling access to education and essential resources.
  • Recognized among local youth-focused initiatives for making a meaningful impact in values-based education and emotional healing.

We’re just getting started — and every life changed is a reminder of why we do what we do.
